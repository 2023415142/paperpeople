About Us

At Paper People, we believe in the magic of paper and the joy of journaling. Founded in February 2021, our journey began with a passion for all things
stationery and a dream to share that love with the world. Starting as an online store, we quickly grew a community of fellow stationery enthusiasts
who cherish the art of writing, journaling, and creativity.

In October 2023, we took a significant step forward by opening our first physical store, creating a cozy space where you can experience the charm of
our products firsthand. Our founder, a dedicated journaler and avid collector of journaling supplies, curates a delightful selection of items including
washi tapes, brush pens, unique markers, premium paper, and adorable stickers.

Whether you’re a seasoned journaler or just beginning your creative journey, Paper People is here to inspire and equip you with the finest stationery
essentials. Explore our online store or visit our physical location to discover a world of creativity, inspiration, and endless possibilities.

Welcome to Paper People – where your stationery dreams come true!
